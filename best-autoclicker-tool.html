The Best Autoclicker Tools Worth Installing in 2026

We tested the most popular autoclicker apps for speed, customization, and safety so you can automate repetitive clicks without risking your system.

🖱️

Autoclickers are small utilities that simulate mouse clicks automatically, useful for repetitive tasks like form testing, grinding in games, or automating clicks in software that lacks a built-in macro feature. The right one should be lightweight, easy to configure, and transparent about what it's doing on your system.

What to Look For

  • Open source or signed builds — autoclickers run with the same permissions as you, so trust matters.
  • Adjustable click intervals — down to the millisecond, with randomization to avoid robotic patterns.
  • Hotkey support — start and stop without alt-tabbing out of a game or app.
  • Low resource usage — it should idle at near-zero CPU when not clicking.

Our Picks

OP Auto Clicker Free

A straightforward, no-install option with a clean interface. Lets you set click position, interval, and click type (single, double, left, right), and supports hotkey toggling. Good first stop for casual use.

GS Auto Clicker Free

Similar feature set to OP Auto Clicker with a slightly more compact UI. Supports recording fixed click positions and repeat counts, making it handy for scripted, repeatable sequences.

Auto Clicker - Murgaa Paid

Adds recording and playback of full mouse movement paths, not just single-point clicks. Useful when a task requires clicking across multiple spots on screen in sequence.

A Quick Word on Safety

Many games and platforms treat automated input as a violation of their terms of service, and some anti-cheat systems flag autoclicker processes outright. Before installing one, check whether the software you intend to use it with permits automation, and only download autoclickers from the developer's official site to avoid bundled adware.
